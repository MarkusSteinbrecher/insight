# POV / Whitepaper Template

**Target length**: 3000-5000 words
**Citation style**: Numbered endnotes with bibliography
**Tone**: Authoritative and analytical — thought leader addressing executive/senior technical audience

## Frontmatter

```yaml
---
title: ""
date: YYYY-MM-DD
draft: true
type: pov
topic: topic-slug
tags: []
summary: ""
author: ""
---
```

## Structure

### Executive Summary (200-300 words)
Concise overview of the paper's thesis, key findings, and recommendations. A senior leader should be able to read only this section and grasp the core argument.

### Introduction (300-400 words)
The challenge or opportunity being addressed. Why this topic matters now. Framing of the paper's scope and approach.

### Current Landscape (500-800 words)
Assessment of the current state. Market data, adoption trends, and prevailing approaches. Establish the baseline understanding.

### Analysis (800-1200 words)
The core analytical section. Present findings organized by theme. Each theme should include:
- The observation or pattern
- Supporting evidence (with numbered citations)
- Interpretation and significance

### Key Insight / Thesis (400-600 words)
The paper's central argument — the non-obvious insight that emerges from the analysis. This is the thought leadership contribution. Make it specific and defensible.

### Implications & Recommendations (500-700 words)
Practical implications organized by audience:
- **For technology leaders**: Strategic considerations
- **For practitioners**: Tactical guidance
- **For the industry**: Broader implications

### Looking Ahead (200-300 words)
Where this topic is heading. Predictions grounded in the analysis. Open questions for further research.

### References
Numbered bibliography of all cited sources.

```markdown
## References

[1] Author. "Title." Publisher, Date. URL
[2] ...
```
